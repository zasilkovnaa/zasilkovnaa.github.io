Further information

Did you know that...

A full third of Hungarians shop online. These 3 million customers have access to just 4,500 e-shops. In terms of competition, this makes Hungary a very good country for expansion.

We recommend to allow cash on delivery

A full 77% of Hungarians prefer cash on delivery. Thanks to the possibility of opening and checking the parcel at the pick-up point, which we offer, you will definitely score with them!

Q&A

  • 80% of parcels arrive within the next day. We do not guarantee this delivery time, but we will do our best to deliver your parcel as soon as possible.

    Yes, you can track your parcel online here: https://tracking.packeta.com/en/tracking/search.

    If you are unable to pick up your parcel, please send us a reply text message and we will extend the storage time to up to 21 days free of charge. You can also extend the storage time in the mobile app Zásilkovna. For Z-Boxes, the storage period is limited. During the Christmas season, the storage time and the possibility of extending it may be temporarily limited.

    We send notifications to customers as soon as we receive information from the e-shop, when the parcel has been accepted for shipping, when the parcel has been delivered to the depot, and when it has been delivered to the pick-up point. The customer receives a tracking number from us, which he/she can use to track the movement of the parcel at https://tracking.packeta.com/en/tracking/search.

    We inform the customer about the delivery of the parcel via email, SMS and notification in the mobile app. The sender of the parcel will also receive information from us about the successful delivery of the parcel.
