Logistics

Logistics is a field that deals with the planning and management of the flow of goods, the services connected with the journey of goods from the manufacturer to the final consumer, as well as the storage of goods. The goal of logistics is for all processes to take place at the right time and for the goods to reach their destination quickly and efficiently. Logistics processes are very complex and involve manufacturing companies, sellers, transporters and government administration. Logistics includes terms such as storage, packaging and packaging technology, forwarding and transportation. A logistics company thus provides a wide range of services — from transport flow management, capacity assurance and route planning, through goods storage and depot management to goods delivery and end customer information.
