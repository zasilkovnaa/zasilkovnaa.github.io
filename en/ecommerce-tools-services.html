Monitor your
competition

The success of your online shop relies not only on a great product portfolio or the perfect marketing strategy. Anyone can do that. Be a step ahead of your competitors and start monitoring them. Now's the time!

Be one step ahead at all times

The competition on the Czech and Slovak market is tremendous. To keep your online shop at the top, it pays off to be informed about what your competitors are doing.

Data from a big part of the market

Price comparison websites such as Heureka.cz, Heureka.sk, or Zbozi.cz are the best source of information on your competitors because they gather data from a large part of the market in one place.

Competition monitoring

Observing your competition

Competition monitoring allows you to be aware of what's happening on the market, discover hidden opportunities, and react swiftly to your competitors ́actions. In order to receive a report, you need to advertise on a price comparison website and have your products paired there.

What can you observe thanks to competition monitoring?

01

What are the product prices of your selected competitors?

02

Who sells products cheaper and for how much?

03

Which of the products you are selling are significantly more expensive than with the competition?

04

How is your stock availability compared to the competition?

05

What is the lowest delivery price your competitors are offering?

06

How many other online shops are selling the same products?

07

At what price level are you and your competitors being shown at comparison websites?

08

How well do your products sell generally on Heureka (so called Heureka TOP products)?

09

Which product info appears on the price comparison sites? (product ratings, the number of product reviews…)
